Krishna Vr

Krishna Valdez Ramírez (Krishna VR) is a visual artist recognized worldwide for her work in photography describing it as pictorial, dark and at the same time romantic; creating characters and atmospheres that express a situation personal or world that he describes as metaphors in his work.

Krishna describes his work as ‘visual art’ although the main base is always photography since this work involves building characters and telling personal and social stories in portraiture.

Krishna Vr's style in a word would be "formidable." Very powerful or strong because of the energy charge and the amount of feelings that each piece of art holds and that tends to inspire fear or wonder.
