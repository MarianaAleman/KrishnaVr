BIOGRAPHY

Krishna Valdez Ramírez (Krishna VR) (May 20 1999, México) is a young photographer also well-known by her creative and conceptual works of art. Krishna demonstrated extraordinary artistic talent in his early years, creating nonexistent characters that usually just existed in her imagination and now she says that melancholic and sad moments are the ‘most inspiring moments is her life’, there it comes the creation of ‘Ofelia’ ,one of her favorite artworks created in 2015 when she was feeling sad about the amount of things happening in her life and got inspired with a friend that also loves art.

About 5 years ago she took her first picture with a professional camera and he felt that photography could be a way to express herself with her own body as she got inspiration from other conceptual photographers on the web. She explains that we can’t create without an idea that comes from the deepest parts of our heart and feelings, ideas come but incomplete when you don’t really feel inspired about what you are doing.

Despite his young age, Krishna has a very wide experience within the artistic world, from exhibitions in the old world in renowned places such as the Louvre Museum, Italy, Amsterdam, Spain where he has been awarded multiple times countries such as China, Japan and the United Arab Emirates. In addition, in Latin America where he constantly participates in congresses and conferences of international stature to share his way of working creatively, managing to inspire young people and adults with his simple way of creating, but captivating with ideas that generate an impact on the mind of the viewer. until achieving an important connection to share, being considered one of the youngest and most influential artists in Mexico.
