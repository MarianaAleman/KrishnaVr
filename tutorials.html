TUTORIALS

KrishnaVr is really interested in share her skills and talent, thas why she gives workshops and tutorials. To help young people to learn new skills. She has two main tutorial which are the following:

VIDEOTUTORIAL ‘JOY’ RETOQUE CREATIVO PARA RETRATO

DURATION: 1hr

Video tutorial lasting 1 hour where you will learn the complete process of 'Joy' photography from developing the file to its final details of color and depth. In this tutorial that you can enjoy the day you like and at your own pace, you will learn to give life to your work in a very creative way.

What will I learn?

  • Revealed
  • Digital makeup
  • Creation of freckles and hair
  • Color Management
  • Integration elements
  • Setting
  • Web Save
  • Eye and skin retouch

Materials included:

1 hour video tutorial, access to exclusive Facebook group to clarify doubts with Krishna VR, RAW AND JPG photos for practice. (Forbidden to use them in a commercial way, only for personal use), brushes and PNG elements to download for free.

VIDEOTUTORIAL RETRATO CONCEPTUAL B&W

DURATION: 1hr

Video tutorial with a duration of 1 hour where you will learn the complete process of a photograph with a dark and black and white setting, from developing the file to its final monochrome details and textures. In this tutorial that you can enjoy the day you like and at your own pace, you will learn to give life to your works with a bizarre setting and you will be able to practice with the included photographs.

What will I learn?

  • Revealed
  • Eye and skin retouch
  • Smoothie
  • Textures and setting
  • Selective correction
  • Black and white
  • Special effects

Materials included:

1 hour video tutorial, access to exclusive Facebook group to clarify doubts with Krishna VR, RAW AND JPG photos for practice. (Forbidden to use them in a commercial way, only for personal use), brushes and PNG elements to download for free.
